Until We Meet Again

Dear Seniors, as you close this beautiful chapter at Vijaya College, Mulki, we want you to know how much you've meant to us. You've been our guides, mentors, and inspiration. From sharing notes to showing us the ropes of college life, you've been pillars of support we could always count on. Though you're moving toward new horizons, the impact you've made will remain forever. We promise to carry forward the legacy of kindness and excellence you've left behind. Here's to your bright futures and the promise that this is just "until we meet again."


From Your Juniors | Class of 2023-2026 | Vijaya College, Mulki
